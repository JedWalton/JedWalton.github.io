Ray Traced Reverberation Chamber

Design and implementation of an artificial reverberation chamber from the ground up with the goal of emulating a truly immersive binaural listening experience when listening with stereo headphones. The ray tracing approach enables a realistic three-dimensional spatial depiction through development of a realistic physical room via implementation of a reverberation chamber powered by ray tracing concepts enabling effective emulation of the propagation of sound from a point sound source to realistic microphones with a selection of pickup patterns.
